About

Due to COVID-19, many of us have been forced to quarantine ourselves, staying in our homes for months at our desks. Why not use these unforgiving times and get motivational about working out to get fit! The WorkIt program gives users scheduled workout videos and specialized workout sessions with a professional instructor.

How To Use

WorkIt is an extremily easy workout website where you work it! You just sign up and choose the workout you want. Once you sign up, you wil recieve a schedule that fits your requests.
